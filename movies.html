Movies

John Wick

Legendary hitman John Wick comes out of retirement when thugs kill his puppy, a final gift from his late wife. This triggers a brutal rampage through the criminal underworld as Wick seeks vengeance. The film is renowned for its stylish action sequences, intricate world-building, and Keanu Reeves' intense performance.

London Has Fallen

When terrorists attack during the British Prime Minister's funeral, Secret Service agent Mike Banning must protect the U.S. President across a chaotic London. This high-octane sequel to Olympus Has Fallen features non-stop action, spectacular explosions, and Gerard Butler's iconic one-liners as he battles through the city's landmarks.
