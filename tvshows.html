TV Shows

The Flash

Barry Allen, a forensic scientist in Central City, gains super-speed abilities after a freak accident. Using his powers to fight crime and protect the innocent, he becomes The Flash - the fastest man alive. The show combines action, drama, and sci-fi elements as Barry battles metahumans and navigates time travel paradoxes.

Supernatural

Brothers Sam and Dean Winchester travel across America hunting supernatural creatures, demons, and ghosts. Following in their father's footsteps, they encounter terrifying monsters while searching for their missing dad and battling the forces of darkness. The show blends horror, fantasy, and drama over 15 thrilling seasons.
