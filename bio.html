CHILDHOOD STORY

1My name is Philip Andrew Paradiang. 2 I grew up in Tondo, Manila, and this is where I was born too. 3 My parents were always supportive, encouraging me to be curious and explore the world around me. 4 I always wanted to play outside, and sometimes I wanted to skip school just to play, but my parents wouldn't let me. 5 When it was my first time going to school, I cried because I didn't want my mom to leave me there. 6 My mom would read bedtime stories to me and my siblings. 7 Even though I was still young, I liked to spend time drawing, making little sketches of the things I saw in anime. 8 I wasn't great at art, but it felt fun and relaxing. 9 School was a place where I could make friends and show what I had learned. 10 I'm not a smart kid, but my teachers were very kind and helped me grow. 11 I love visiting my grandparents because they're so kind to me, and I love them. 12 I also spent a lot of time with my friends from the neighborhood. 13 We would ride our bikes around the block or play hide and seek outside. 14 Our days were filled with simple games like luksong baka, hide and seek, and tumbang preso. 15 On rainy days, I was happy when the class got suspended and I got to watch cartoons all day. 16 The town I grew up in was lively, and I still cherish the friendships I made here. 17 My childhood was a time of exploration, learning, and laying the foundation for my future. 18 My parents always found a way to give us opportunities to learn and grow. 19 Those moments made me feel happy and safe, and they helped me grow into who I am today. 20 Looking back, I realize how much those small moments shaped my love for nature, learning, and being creative.
